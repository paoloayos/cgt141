Game Design and Development

For this major, you are going to need a strong computer to do all your course work. You are going to be doing a lot of 3d modeling, rendering and generally things that will need a lot of processing power.

The Budget options will get the job done, but going for something that is little under specifications may cause some performance problems that may hinder your work flow. These are the options if you need anything to do your course work.

The Recomeded options should be plenty to run all the programs you need. There are a few more things like a higher quality screen, more RAM and more storage that would be nice but really just add up to be expensive.   

The Premium options are perfect for those who have the money and want a hiccup free technology experience that is 100% guaranteed to last all the way thru your college years and eventually your professional life. The more money you put into your computer the better.
